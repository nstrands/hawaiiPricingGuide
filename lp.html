Achieve Hawaii for SBA Success

Achieve3000® offers the only cloud-based solutions that deliver daily differentiated nonfiction reading and writing instruction based on each student's individual Lexile® reading level. Featuring a custom curriculum aligned with Hawaii standards, Achieve Hawaii ensures all learners receive the targeted instruction and daily practice they need for SBA success! Includes:

  • LevelSet™ assessment: Initial assessment and ongoing progress monitoring
  • Alignment to McGraw-Hill Reading Wonders® (grades 2-5) and the College Board's SpringBoard® (grades 6-12)
  • Built-in Workforce Readiness curriculum
  • Leadership Edition: Data dashboard helps administrators monitor the success of their implementation
  • Home Edition: Parents and guardians enjoy resources in 19 languages, including Hawaiian
  • Anywhere, anytime, any-device access: Available 24/7 for students, teachers, and administrators – including offline access!
  • Coming in 2016! All-new Hawaii SBA ELA practice courses with new technology-enhanced items – included at each grade level at no additional cost!

Achieve Hawaii is available with Pro, the literacy solution for your mixed-ability English language arts, science, and social studies classrooms; Boost, an intensive Response to Intervention solution; Access, a targeted English language learner solution; and Español, a flexible Spanish literacy solution for beginners and native speakers alike. Keep reading to learn about these exciting new solutions, and how you can see exceptional scores on SBA!

Pro: Reach All Learners Across the Curriculum

For Hawaii's English language arts, science, and social studies classes with a mix of student abilities or diverse student populations, Pro is the differentiated literacy solution that accelerates reading gains for all students – all in a single classroom. With robust supports for at-risk readers, linguistic scaffolds for English language learners, and additional challenges for talented and gifted students, Pro prepares all students for success in school and beyond!

Built for Hawaii: The Achieve3000 Pro Hawaii Edition features a custom strategic curriculum aligned to Hawaii's grade-level standards for language arts, science, and social studies. With targeted, daily SBA practice that includes technology-enhanced items in every lesson, the Pro Hawaii Edition ensures all Hawaii students are fully prepared for test day.

Smarty Ants: The Right Start for Young Learners

The path to college and career literacy begins with Smarty Ants®, an effective, research-driven solution that differentiates instruction in foundational reading skills and accelerates student achievement – all in an engaging, interactive, online learning environment. With an adaptive content system that constantly measures student progress and tailors instruction accordingly, robust learning scaffolds embedded in every lesson, and dozens of engaging, educational games to play, Smarty Ants ensures all students stay motivated and moving forward on the road to reading success!

Built for Hawaii: Smarty Ants is fully aligned with the Common Core State Standards, ensuring all Hawaii students master the essential phonemic awareness, phonics, fluency, vocabulary, and comprehension skills needed to become confident, capable readers ready to conquer complex, grade-level text.

Boost: Targeted Intervention and Special Education

For targeted and intensive intervention, Boost provides the right combination of differentiated instruction, effective learning scaffolds, and instructional frameworks to accelerate reading gains in your Tier II and Tier III intervention and special education models. With robust supports built into every lesson, custom routines focused on developing academic and cross-disciplinary vocabulary, and additional courses in foundational reading skills such as phonics and fluency, Boost helps all below-level readers reach their full potential!

Built for Hawaii: The Achieve3000 Boost Hawaii Edition is the only patented and proven differentiated instruction solution that ensures intervention and special education students receive instruction aligned to Hawaii's grade-level standards, plus daily SBA practice with online activities and technology-enhanced items!

Access: Accelerating English Language Acquisition

Whether in small pull-out groups or in specialized classrooms, Access accelerates language and literacy gains for every English language learner, from beginner to advanced, and prepares them for success across the entire curriculum. A key feature is Access's targeted student routine, which introduces and reinforces key learning strategies, develops academic vocabulary in both English and Spanish, builds oral fluency, and guides students as they review and edit their own writing.

Built for Hawaii: The Achieve3000 Access Hawaii Edition is the only patented and proven differentiated instruction solution that provides English language learners with equitable access to grade-level instruction aligned to Hawaii's grade-level standards, plus daily SBA practice – all with the linguistic supports they need to succeed!

Español: Building Literacy Skills in Spanish

For Spanish immersion, dual language, and bilingual classrooms, Español ensures all learners can improve their Spanish-language proficiency while simultaneously building transferrable literacy skills, content-area knowledge and academic vocabulary. Designed for Spanish-speaking students of every ability level, from beginner to native speaker, Español provides the robust learning scaffolds, targeted instruction, and tailored student routine that empower every learner to ¡tener éxito!

Built for Hawaii: The Achieve3000 Español Hawaii Edition is the only patented and proven differentiated instruction solution that provides a full strategic custom strategic curriculum aligned to Hawaii's grade-level standards, plus daily practice for the SBA with technology-enhanced items and online activities – all in Spanish!
